# TV & Streaming Advertising for Remodelers

Remodeling companies sell transformations that change how families live in their homes. A cramped, dated kitchen becomes a spacious, modern gathering place. A tired master bathroom becomes a spa-like retreat. A dark, chopped-up floor plan opens into a bright, flowing living space. These transformations deserve to be seen on the biggest screen in the house, not buried on page three of Google search results.

Streaming TV advertising puts your best work in front of homeowners across your service area. The visual impact of a kitchen reveal on a 65-inch television creates desire that no Google Ad or SEO listing can match. Homeowners watch your transformation unfold and imagine their own home looking that good. That desire becomes a phone call. That phone call becomes a $50,000 contract.

ServiceLine Pro delivers connected TV advertising for remodeling companies through Spectrum Reach with no agency fee on your media spend. We handle creative production, audience targeting, and campaign management while your budget works exclusively on reaching the homeowners who matter.

## The Remodeling Company's Marketing Challenge

Remodeling companies face a marketing challenge that most home service businesses do not. Your projects cost $20,000 to $200,000. Your sales cycle spans weeks to months. Homeowners get three to five quotes and compare portfolios, references, and timelines before making a decision. The trust required to hand someone the keys to your home for a three-month renovation is enormous.

Google Ads and SEO reach homeowners who are actively searching for a remodeler. These are valuable leads, but they are cold. The homeowner has never heard of you. They are comparing you against every other listing on the page. Price and speed of response often determine who gets the first appointment, regardless of quality.

Streaming TV changes the competitive dynamic. A homeowner who has seen your before-and-after transformations on Hulu six times over the past two months walks into the estimate meeting with pre-built trust. They have already seen the quality of your work. They already associate your brand with beautiful results. The conversation starts from a completely different place than it does with a cold Google lead.

This pre-built trust translates into measurable business results: higher close rates, higher average project values, and less price negotiation. Homeowners pay premium prices to the remodeler they trust.

## Showcasing Transformations on the Big Screen

Remodeling is one of the few industries where the product itself is inherently cinematic. A kitchen remodel reveal, with the camera panning from the cramped, dated before-state to the stunning, sunlit after-state, creates an emotional response that text and static images cannot replicate.

Streaming TV gives you a platform to showcase these transformations at scale. Instead of a handful of visitors clicking through your website portfolio, 50,000 to 200,000 households see your best work on their television screen. The visual impact is orders of magnitude greater.

### Creative Production for Remodelers

We produce broadcast-quality commercials designed to maximize the impact of your portfolio:

**The Reveal Sequence.** The commercial opens on a dated space. Quick cuts show your team in action: demolition, framing, plumbing and electrical rough-in, drywall, trim work, and finishing. Then the reveal: a slow pan across the finished space, bright and beautiful, with the homeowner's reaction captured on camera. This narrative arc mirrors the HGTV format that millions of homeowners already love.

**Detail Shots.** Close-ups of craftsmanship details sell the quality: custom tile patterns, furniture-grade cabinetry, seamless countertop seams, built-in shelving, and architectural trim work. These details differentiate your work from production-grade competitors.

**Customer Testimonial.** A 10-second testimonial from a homeowner describing their experience and their love for the finished space adds social proof that validates the visual evidence.

**Credibility Layer.** Your years in business, certifications (NARI Certified Remodeler, NKBA membership, EPA Lead-Safe certification), design awards, and manufacturer partnerships appear as text overlays throughout the spot.

**Call to Action.** End with your phone number, website, and a directive: "Schedule your free design consultation" or "See what we can do for your home."

### Seasonal Creative Rotation

We produce multiple versions of your commercial to align with seasonal demand patterns:

**Spring/Summer:** Outdoor living spaces, deck additions, and exterior renovations that homeowners want completed before summer entertaining season.

**Fall/Winter:** Kitchen remodels, bathroom renovations, and interior projects that homeowners plan during cooler months when they are spending more time indoors.

Rotating creative keeps your messaging fresh and relevant throughout the year.

## Targeting Homeowners Ready to Remodel

Spectrum Reach's data enables precision targeting that focuses your budget on the households most likely to invest in remodeling.

### Home Characteristics

**Home age.** Homes aged 15 to 30 years are in the prime remodel window. The original kitchen and bathrooms are dated. The floor plan may not suit how the family uses the space today. These homes represent the highest concentration of remodeling demand.

**Home value.** Homes valued above $300,000 are more likely to receive major renovations because the homeowner has equity, a financial motivation to maintain or increase property value, and the income to invest in quality work.

**Home size.** Larger homes with 2,500-plus square feet tend to have higher remodeling budgets and more complex projects.

### Household Demographics

**Income.** Households with incomes above $100,000 can afford major remodeling projects and are more likely to hire professionals rather than attempt DIY renovations.

**Age.** Homeowners aged 35 to 60 are the primary remodeling demographic. Younger homeowners in this range are upgrading starter homes. Older homeowners are aging in place and modifying their homes for long-term livability.

**Length of ownership.** Homeowners who have lived in their home for 10-plus years have accumulated the equity and the frustration with outdated spaces that drive remodeling decisions.

### Behavioral Targeting

Spectrum Reach identifies households engaging with home design content, renovation shows, Houzz, Pinterest, and interior design publications. These behavioral signals indicate active interest in remodeling, making these households significantly more receptive to your commercial.

### Geographic Focus

Your ads run only in the zip codes you serve. We can further concentrate spending on neighborhoods known for remodeling activity, such as established neighborhoods with mid-century homes, historic districts undergoing revitalization, and affluent suburbs where keeping up with neighborhood standards drives renovation investment.

## Streaming TV and the Remodeling Sales Cycle

Remodeling has the longest sales cycle in home services. From initial inquiry to signed contract, the process typically spans two to eight weeks for major projects. Streaming TV advertising influences this cycle at every stage.

### Awareness

Streaming TV introduces your brand to homeowners who are not yet actively searching for a remodeler. They are in the dreaming phase, imagining what their kitchen could look like, browsing Pinterest boards, watching renovation shows. Your commercial enters this dream space and attaches your company to the transformation they are envisioning.

### Consideration

When the homeowner transitions from dreaming to planning, your brand is already in the consideration set. They do not start from scratch on Google. They search for your company by name or include you automatically in their shortlist of companies to contact.

### Decision

During the estimate and comparison phase, brand familiarity gives you an advantage. The homeowner already feels like they know your company. They have seen your work on their television. The estimate meeting starts from a position of trust rather than skepticism.

### Retention

Homeowners who had a positive remodeling experience and continue seeing your commercials after the project are more likely to hire you again for future projects and to refer you to friends and neighbors. Streaming TV keeps your brand top of mind long after the project is complete.
