# Google Ads for Pest Control Companies

Pest control is a volume business. You need a full route board of recurring customers and a steady stream of one-time service calls to maintain profitability. Google Ads fills both pipelines when campaigns are structured around how homeowners actually search for pest solutions.

The economics of pest control PPC are favorable compared to most home service trades. CPCs average $8 to $25, well below the $30 to $145 range that plumbers and HVAC companies face. This lower cost per click means pest control companies can generate leads affordably enough to achieve positive ROI even on lower-ticket services like general pest treatments ($150 to $300 per visit).

The real profit in pest control PPC comes from converting one-time leads into recurring service plan members. A $50 lead for a $200 ant treatment might look marginal on its own. But if that customer signs up for a $49/month recurring plan, the first-year revenue is $588 with lifetime value extending to $2,000 to $3,000 over 4 to 5 years of retention. Every campaign you build should include a path from initial treatment to recurring plan enrollment.

## Campaign Architecture by Pest Type

Different pests generate different search behavior, different urgency levels, and different ticket values. Campaign separation by pest type lets you manage each category optimally.

### Campaign 1: General Pest Control

Targets homeowners with common pest problems: ants, spiders, cockroaches, and general indoor/outdoor pest pressure.

**Keywords:**
- "Pest control near me"
- "Exterminator near me"
- "Ant exterminator"
- "Spider control"
- "Cockroach exterminator"
- "Bug spray service"
- "Home pest control"

**Bid strategy:** Maximize conversions with target CPA of $30 to $50. General pest treatment tickets average $150 to $300, and the primary value is converting these leads to recurring plans.

**Ad copy:** Lead with the specific pest when your keyword triggers are specific. "Ant Problem? Same-Day Treatment. $149 Initial Service. Recurring Plans from $39/Month." For broader terms like "pest control near me," lead with trust and value: "Licensed Pest Control. Same-Day Service. Free Inspection. Satisfaction Guaranteed."

**Landing page:** List the pests you treat with photos and brief descriptions. Include a special offer for new customers. Prominently feature your recurring plan with pricing. Short form with pest type dropdown, address, and phone number.

### Campaign 2: Termite Services

Termite keywords carry the highest CPCs and highest ticket values in pest control.

**Keywords:**
- "Termite inspection near me"
- "Termite treatment cost"
- "Termite exterminator"
- "Termite control"
- "WDO inspection"
- "Termite bond"
- "Termite damage repair"

**Bid strategy:** Target CPA of $75 to $150. Termite treatment averages $1,500 to $3,000, and termite bonds generate $150 to $300 in annual recurring revenue per property. The lifetime value of a termite customer justifies aggressive bidding.

**Ad copy:** Expertise and guarantees. "Termite Experts. Free Inspection. Treatment From $1,500. Re-Treatment Guarantee. Licensed & Insured." For real estate searches: "WDO Inspection. 24-Hour Report Turnaround. Real Estate Agent Preferred Provider."

**Landing page:** Dedicated termite page with treatment options (liquid barrier, bait stations, fumigation), pricing, the inspection process, warranty information, and a scheduling form. Include a section for real estate professionals with WDO report details and turnaround times.

### Campaign 3: Rodent Control

Rodent searches peak in fall and winter but maintain steady volume year-round in most markets.

**Keywords:**
- "Mouse exterminator near me"
- "Rat control"
- "Rodent removal"
- "Mice in attic"
- "Rat in house"
- "Rodent exclusion"
- "Animal in attic"

**Bid strategy:** Maximize conversions. Rodent service tickets average $200 to $600 for trapping and exclusion, with potential for larger exclusion projects ($500 to $2,000).

**Ad copy:** Address the urgency and the health concern. "Mice or Rats? We Remove Them Fast. Same-Day Service. Exclusion to Keep Them Out. Free Inspection." Homeowners with rodent problems feel a visceral urgency and respond to messaging that promises quick resolution.

### Campaign 4: Mosquito Treatment

Seasonal campaign active from April through October in most markets.

**Keywords:**
- "Mosquito control near me"
- "Mosquito treatment for yard"
- "Mosquito spraying service"
- "Mosquito misting system"
- "Mosquito barrier treatment"

**Bid strategy:** Maximize conversions with seasonal budget pacing. Mosquito treatment averages $75 to $200 per application, but most customers purchase multi-visit seasonal packages ($400 to $800 total).

**Ad copy:** Outdoor living and protection. "Take Your Yard Back From Mosquitoes. Monthly Treatment Plans. First Treatment $79. Satisfaction Guaranteed."

### Campaign 5: Bed Bug Treatment

Bed bug searches happen year-round with a slight summer peak due to travel season.

**Keywords:**
- "Bed bug treatment near me"
- "Bed bug exterminator"
- "Bed bug heat treatment"
- "Bed bug inspection"
- "How to get rid of bed bugs"

**Bid strategy:** Target CPA of $50 to $100. Bed bug treatment averages $500 to $2,000 per treatment depending on the method (chemical vs heat treatment) and severity.

**Ad copy:** Discretion and effectiveness. "Bed Bug Treatment. Discreet Service. Heat Treatment Available. Same-Day Inspection. Guaranteed Results." Bed bug customers are embarrassed and stressed. Messaging that emphasizes privacy and guaranteed results resonates.

## Seasonal Bid Management

Pest control requires the most aggressive seasonal bid adjustments of any home service trade.

### Budget Calendar

**January-February:** 50% of peak budget. Focus on rodent campaigns and termite inspection campaigns (pre-swarming season).

**March-April:** 80% of peak budget. Ramp up general pest and termite campaigns as temperatures rise and pest activity increases.

**May-June:** 100% peak budget. All campaigns active with maximum bids. General pest, termite, and mosquito searches hit peak volume.

**July-August:** 100% peak budget. Maintain all summer campaigns. Bed bug searches peak with travel season.

**September-October:** 80% of peak budget. Reduce mosquito and summer pest campaigns. Increase rodent campaign budgets as fall drives rodents indoors.

**November-December:** 50% of peak budget. Focus on rodent campaigns. Maintain minimal general pest and termite presence.

## Recurring Service Keywords

Dedicate a portion of your budget to keywords that attract customers seeking ongoing pest control.

**Keywords:**
- "Monthly pest control service"
- "Pest control plan"
- "Quarterly pest control"
- "Pest control subscription"
- "Annual pest control service"
- "Preventive pest control"

**Landing page:** Tier comparison (monthly, bi-monthly, quarterly) with pricing. Emphasis on the value compared to one-time reactive treatments. Online signup functionality. Money-back guarantee on initial service.

These keywords attract homeowners who already understand they need ongoing pest management. They are pre-sold on the recurring model and convert at higher rates than homeowners searching for a specific pest problem.

## Landing Page Strategy

### General Pest Landing Page

- Hero: "Pest-Free Home Guaranteed. Same-Day Service Available."
- Pest gallery showing common pests you treat with brief descriptions
- New customer offer ($149 initial treatment, $99 with recurring plan signup)
- Recurring plan comparison (monthly vs quarterly)
- Trust signals: licensed, insured, satisfaction guarantee
- Short form: address, pest type (dropdown), phone number
- Reviews section

### Termite Landing Page

- Hero: "Free Termite Inspection. Expert Treatment From $1,500."
- Treatment options comparison (Sentricon, Termidor, fumigation)
- The inspection and treatment process
- Warranty and re-treatment guarantee details
- Real estate section with WDO report information
- Scheduling form with date picker

### Rodent Landing Page

- Hero: "Rats or Mice? Fast Removal. Guaranteed Exclusion."
- Services covered: trapping, removal, exclusion, cleanup
- How exclusion works (sealing entry points)
- Pricing ranges
- Before-and-after exclusion photos
- Emergency scheduling form

## Call Tracking and Revenue Attribution

Track every lead from click to service completion:

- Dynamic call tracking numbers on each landing page
- Call recording for lead quality assessment
- CRM integration to track one-time customers who convert to recurring plans
- Revenue attribution by campaign and keyword
- Lifetime value tracking for recurring plan customers acquired through PPC

The most important metric for pest control PPC is not cost per lead. It is cost per recurring plan acquisition. A campaign that generates $50 leads but converts 30% to recurring plans at $50/month is worth dramatically more than a campaign generating $30 leads with zero recurring conversions.

## Negative Keywords

**Immediate additions:**
- DIY, home remedy, natural, organic, vinegar, essential oil
- Jobs, career, hiring, salary, technician
- Free (unless running a free inspection offer)
- Products, spray, trap, bait (retail product shoppers)
- Lawn care, landscaping (separate industry)
- Pet safe (add if your treatments are pet-safe and you want these clicks; exclude if this triggers price-sensitive comparison shopping)

**Ongoing management:** "Pest" triggers searches for "pest" in gaming contexts, "pest analysis" in business contexts, and various irrelevant uses. "Bug" triggers software bug, VW Bug, bug-out bag, and countless other irrelevant searches. Monitor search terms reports weekly during peak season and bi-weekly during off-season.

## Retargeting

Pest control retargeting works particularly well for two scenarios:

**One-time to recurring conversion.** Retarget past customers who received one-time treatments but did not sign up for recurring plans. Message: "Pests come back. Your protection should not stop. Recurring plans from $39/month."

**Termite research to decision.** Homeowners researching termite treatment often take 1 to 2 weeks to decide. Retarget visitors to your termite page with messaging reinforcing your warranty and free inspection offer.
