# Google Ads for Painting Companies

Painting companies operate in one of the most favorable Google Ads environments in home services. CPCs are moderate ($8 to $25), customer ticket values are substantial ($1,500 to $8,000 for most residential projects), and the visual nature of painting allows for highly engaging ad creative that drives click-through rates above industry averages.

The challenge with painting PPC is seasonality and lead qualification. Exterior painting demand compresses into a 6 to 7 month window. Interior painting stays steadier but generates smaller project sizes. Without smart seasonal budget management and landing page design that pre-qualifies leads, painting companies either underspend during peak season (missing revenue) or overspend during slow months (wasting budget).

This guide covers campaign structure, seasonal strategy, and conversion optimization specifically for painting contractors running Google Ads.

## Campaign Structure

### Campaign 1: Exterior Residential Painting

Your highest-ticket campaign targeting homeowners who need their homes painted.

**Keywords:**
- "House painter near me"
- "Exterior painter [city]"
- "Exterior house painting cost"
- "House painting [city]"
- "Exterior painting contractor"
- "Stucco painter"
- "Siding painter"

**Bid strategy:** Target CPA of $50 to $120. Exterior residential painting projects average $3,000 to $8,000. A $80 cost per lead with a 25% close rate produces a $320 cost per job on a $5,000 average ticket.

**Ad copy:** Visual quality and professionalism. "Professional House Painters. Premium Products. Detailed Prep. 5-Year Warranty. Free Exterior Estimate." Homeowners hiring exterior painters prioritize quality over price because the result is visible to their entire neighborhood.

**Landing page:** Photo-heavy gallery of completed exterior projects. Pricing ranges by home size. Prep process description. Paint brand information (Sherwin-Williams Duration, Benjamin Moore Aura). Free estimate form.

### Campaign 2: Interior Residential Painting

Targets homeowners planning interior painting projects. Lower average ticket but consistent year-round demand.

**Keywords:**
- "Interior painter near me"
- "Room painting service"
- "Interior painting cost"
- "Wall painter [city]"
- "Interior house painter"

**Bid strategy:** Target CPA of $30 to $60. Interior painting projects range from $500 for a single room to $5,000+ for whole-home repaints. Budget allocation should account for the variance in project sizes.

**Ad copy:** Convenience and cleanliness. "Interior Painters. We Move Furniture, Protect Floors, and Leave No Mess. Free Color Consultation. Same-Week Scheduling." Interior painting customers worry about disruption to their daily life.

### Campaign 3: Cabinet Painting

Cabinet painting is a high-growth niche with excellent Google Ads economics. Homeowners spend $3,000 to $7,000 on cabinet painting instead of $15,000 to $40,000 on new cabinets.

**Keywords:**
- "Cabinet painting near me"
- "Kitchen cabinet painting"
- "Cabinet refinishing"
- "Kitchen cabinet painting cost"
- "Cabinet painter"

**Bid strategy:** Target CPA of $75 to $150. Cabinet painting projects average $2,500 to $5,000. These leads are highly qualified because homeowners have already decided on cabinet painting specifically.

**Ad copy:** Transformation and value. "Transform Your Kitchen. Professional Cabinet Painting From $2,500. Premium Finish. No Sanding. 5-Year Warranty." The value proposition vs new cabinets should be clear.

**Landing page:** Before-and-after cabinet photos (6 to 8 examples), process overview, pricing, timeline (typically 5 to 7 days), and a gallery showing various color transformations (dark to white, oak to gray, etc.).

### Campaign 4: Commercial Painting

Targets property managers, business owners, and commercial clients.

**Keywords:**
- "Commercial painter near me"
- "Commercial painting contractor"
- "Office painting service"
- "Industrial painting"
- "Apartment painting service"
- "Property management painting"

**Bid strategy:** Target CPA of $100 to $200. Commercial painting projects range from $2,000 for a small office to $50,000+ for multi-unit complexes.

**Ad copy:** Credentials and minimal disruption. "Commercial Painting Contractor. Licensed & Insured. After-Hours Available. Minimal Business Disruption. Free Bid."

## Targeting Homeowners

Google Ads offers demographic and audience targeting that helps painting companies reach their ideal customers.

### Demographic Targeting

**Income targeting:** Painting is a service primarily purchased by homeowners with disposable income. Target the top 50% of household income brackets in your market. This reduces wasted spend on renters and budget-constrained searchers.

**Homeowner status:** Google allows targeting users identified as homeowners. Apply this as a bid adjustment (increase bids 20% for homeowners) rather than an exclusion, so you do not lose visibility entirely for renters who may be authorized to hire painters.

**Age targeting:** Painting customers typically skew 30 to 65. Reduce bids for the 18 to 24 demographic, which is less likely to be hiring residential painters.

### Audience Targeting

**In-Market audiences:** Target users Google identifies as in-market for home improvement and painting services. These users have demonstrated through their browsing behavior that they are actively researching home improvement.

**Custom intent audiences:** Create custom audiences based on URLs visitors have browsed (competitor painting company websites, paint manufacturer color tools, home design inspiration sites like Houzz).

## Seasonal Bid Adjustments

### Spring Ramp-Up (March-April)

Increase bids 20 to 30 percent as exterior painting demand begins. This is when homeowners who delayed fall painting projects and those who made spring painting resolutions enter the market. Competition increases but so does conversion rates.

### Peak Summer (May-August)

Maintain elevated bids for exterior campaigns. Monitor impression share weekly. If your impression share drops below 70% for top keywords, increase bids to maintain visibility. During peak season, every lost impression is a potential $5,000 job going to a competitor.

### Fall Transition (September-October)

Shift budget from exterior to interior campaigns. Reduce exterior bids by 20% and increase interior and cabinet painting bids by 20%. Fall is prime cabinet painting season as homeowners want updated kitchens for the holidays.

### Winter (November-February)

Reduce total ad spend by 30 to 50% unless you do significant interior and cabinet work. Maintain interior and cabinet painting campaigns. Pause or heavily reduce exterior campaigns except in warm-climate markets where exterior painting is year-round.

## Landing Page Optimization

### Photo Gallery Priority

Painting landing pages live and die by their photo galleries. The gallery is the most viewed element on the page and has the greatest impact on conversion rate.

**Gallery requirements:**
- 8 to 12 before-and-after photo pairs
- Mix of project types (exterior, interior, cabinets, trim)
- Consistent photo quality (same lighting, same angle for before and after)
- Color transformation examples (dark to light, dated to modern)
- Brief descriptions with neighborhood names and paint colors used

**Gallery placement:** Above the fold or immediately below the hero section. Do not bury the gallery behind walls of text. Homeowners hiring painters want to see the work before reading anything.

### Pricing Transparency

Include starting prices on your landing page:
- "Single room interior painting from $350"
- "Exterior home painting from $2,800"
- "Cabinet painting from $2,500"

This pre-qualifies visitors and increases lead quality. Homeowners who see your pricing and still request an estimate are prepared for your rates.

### Estimate Request Form

Keep forms simple:
- Name
- Phone
- Address (or zip code)
- Project type (dropdown: exterior, interior, cabinets, commercial, other)
- Approximate scope (dropdown: 1 room, 2-3 rooms, whole house, exterior, cabinets)
- Preferred start date

### Trust Elements

- License and insurance details
- Paint brand partnerships (Benjamin Moore, Sherwin-Williams logos)
- Review count and star rating
- Portfolio link (for visitors who want to see more work)
- Warranty information

## Negative Keywords

**Immediate additions:**
- DIY, how to, tutorial, tips
- Jobs, career, hiring, salary
- Supplies, paint, roller, brush, tape (retail shoppers)
- Free
- Art, artist, face painting, body painting
- Car, auto, vehicle (auto painting)
- Bob Ross (painting instruction searches)
- Class, course, lesson (painting classes)

**Ongoing management:** "Painting" triggers an enormous range of irrelevant searches from art-related queries to nail painting to painting video games. Review search terms weekly during your first three months and monthly thereafter.

## Retargeting for Painting Companies

Painting decisions involve a research phase of 1 to 3 weeks for most homeowners. Retargeting keeps your company visible during this period.

**Retargeting audiences and messaging:**
- Exterior page visitors: Before-and-after exterior photos with free estimate CTA
- Interior page visitors: Interior transformation photos with seasonal messaging
- Cabinet painting page visitors: Cabinet before-and-after with pricing and timeline
- Estimate form abandoners: "Still planning your painting project? Get your free estimate in 24 hours."

**Retargeting windows:**
- Exterior painting visitors: 30 days
- Interior painting visitors: 21 days
- Cabinet painting visitors: 45 days (longer decision cycle for kitchen projects)
