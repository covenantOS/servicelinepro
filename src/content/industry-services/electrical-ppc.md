# Google Ads for Electricians

Electrical contractors have a significant advantage in Google Ads that most do not realize: their keyword costs are lower than competing trades. While plumbers pay $35 to $55 per click and HVAC companies pay $50 to $145, electricians average $10 to $30 per click for most service keywords. Emergency electrical terms run higher at $25 to $45, but even these are below the plumbing and HVAC equivalents.

This cost advantage exists because fewer electrical contractors run Google Ads compared to plumbers and HVAC companies. The auction has less competition, which keeps bid prices lower. For electrical contractors willing to invest in well-managed campaigns, this gap represents an opportunity to generate leads at CPCs that more competitive trades can only dream of.

The diversity of electrical services creates another advantage. You can run campaigns for emergency repairs, panel upgrades, EV charger installations, generator installations, and commercial work simultaneously. Each campaign targets a distinct audience with different intent and different budget, allowing you to optimize for the highest-value leads across multiple revenue streams.

## Campaign Structure for Electrical Contractors

### Campaign 1: Emergency Electrical

Emergency electrical searches convert at the highest rates of any electrical keyword category. Homeowners experiencing power loss, sparking outlets, or burning smells need help immediately.

**Keywords:**
- "Emergency electrician near me"
- "Electrician open now"
- "No power in house"
- "Sparking outlet repair"
- "Electrical burning smell"
- "Breaker keeps tripping"
- "Power outage just my house"

**Bid strategy:** Target impression share at 85%+ for top-of-page positions. Emergency electrical searches happen unpredictably, and being visible matters more than cost efficiency during these moments.

**Ad copy:** Availability and safety. "Emergency Electrician. Available Now. Licensed & Insured. Your Safety Is Our Priority. Call (555) 123-4567." Electrical emergencies involve genuine safety concerns (fire risk, shock hazard), so messaging that acknowledges the danger and communicates professional capability resonates.

**Landing page:** Phone-number-dominant design. Above the fold: phone number, "Available Now" messaging, license number, and three trust signals. Below: common electrical emergencies listed, what to do while waiting for the electrician (safety instructions), and testimonials from emergency call customers.

### Campaign 2: Panel Upgrades

Panel upgrades represent the highest-ticket residential electrical service. These leads are worth the investment in targeted campaigns.

**Keywords:**
- "Electrical panel upgrade"
- "200 amp panel upgrade cost"
- "Breaker panel replacement"
- "Fuse box replacement"
- "Electrical service upgrade"
- "Federal Pacific panel replacement"
- "Zinsco panel replacement"
- "100 to 200 amp upgrade"

**Bid strategy:** Target CPA with a ceiling of $100 to $200. Panel upgrades average $1,800 to $4,000, making a $150 cost per lead highly profitable at typical close rates.

**Ad copy:** Address the specific trigger. For general panel upgrade searches: "Electrical Panel Upgrade. Licensed Master Electrician. Same-Week Installation. Permits & Inspections Included." For hazardous panel brand searches: "Federal Pacific Panel? Free Safety Inspection. Replacement From $1,800. Licensed & Insured."

**Landing page:** Educational content about why panels need upgrading, which panel brands are dangerous, signs of an overloaded panel, the upgrade process, and pricing. Include photos of neat, professional panel installations versus old, corroded panels.

### Campaign 3: EV Charger Installation

EV charger demand grows every quarter. Early investment in EV charger PPC builds market position before competition intensifies.

**Keywords:**
- "EV charger installation near me"
- "Level 2 EV charger install"
- "Tesla Wall Connector installation"
- "Home EV charger electrician"
- "EV charger install cost"
- "240V outlet for electric car"
- "ChargePoint home installation"

**Bid strategy:** Maximize conversions. EV charger CPCs are moderate ($8 to $20), and the customer profile is high-income and tech-savvy. Smart bidding performs well because the conversion patterns are consistent.

**Ad copy:** Technical competence and convenience. "EV Charger Installation by Licensed Electricians. All Brands: Tesla, ChargePoint, JuiceBox. Panel Assessment Included. Same-Week Install." EV owners want assurance that the electrician understands EV charging technology, not just general electrical work.

**Landing page:** Dedicated EV charger page covering charger brands you install, the installation process, panel upgrade requirements, pricing, and timeline. Include photos of clean charger installations in residential garages.

### Campaign 4: Generator Installation

Generator installations produce premium leads with high job values and motivated buyers.

**Keywords:**
- "Whole house generator installation"
- "Generac generator installer"
- "Standby generator cost"
- "Home generator electrician"
- "Automatic transfer switch installation"
- "Kohler generator installation"

**Bid strategy:** Manual CPC with aggressive bids on top-of-page positions. Generator installations average $5,000 to $15,000, justifying CPCs of $30 to $50.

**Ad copy:** Manufacturer credentials and turnkey service. "Authorized Generac Installer. Whole-Home Generator Installation. Permits, Gas Line, and Electrical Included. Free In-Home Estimate."

**Landing page:** Generator-focused page with brand comparisons (Generac, Kohler, Briggs & Stratton), sizing guidance, installation process, pricing tiers, and financing options. Include photos of completed generator installations.

### Campaign 5: Commercial Electrical (Optional)

If your business serves commercial clients, run a separate campaign targeting commercial searches.

**Keywords:**
- "Commercial electrician near me"
- "Electrical contractor for business"
- "Office lighting installation"
- "LED lighting retrofit"
- "Commercial electrical maintenance"
- "Tenant buildout electrician"

**Bid strategy:** Target CPA with a flexible ceiling. Commercial leads vary widely in project value ($2,000 to $100,000+). Smart bidding needs time to learn which commercial leads convert to high-value projects.

**Ad copy:** Credentials and experience. "Licensed Commercial Electrician. Tenant Buildouts, Lighting Retrofits, Maintenance Contracts. Fully Insured. References Available."

## Negative Keyword Strategy for Electricians

Electrical keywords attract substantial irrelevant traffic.

**Immediate negative keywords:**
- DIY, how to, tutorial, youtube
- Jobs, career, hiring, apprentice, journeyman, trade school
- Parts, supply, wire, breaker (parts shoppers)
- Guitar, bass (electric guitar searches)
- Car, auto, vehicle (auto electrical)
- Utility, power company, electric company (utility searches)
- Bill, rate, cost per kwh (electricity bill searches)
- Free
- Commercial (if residential only) or Residential (if commercial only)

**Ongoing management:** "Electrical" triggers an extraordinary range of irrelevant searches: "electrical engineering," "electrical tape," "electrical outlet cover," "electrical fire cause." Review search terms weekly for the first 90 days and monthly thereafter.

## Seasonal Strategy for Electrical PPC

Electrical demand follows seasonal patterns tied to weather, home buying, and construction cycles.

**Spring:** Home buying season drives panel upgrade and home inspection searches. Outdoor lighting demand increases. Bid up on panel upgrade, outlet, and lighting keywords.

**Summer:** Peak construction and renovation season. Generator demand increases in storm-prone markets. EV charger installations peak as new vehicle deliveries are highest. AC-related electrical calls increase (dedicated circuits, panel capacity for new AC units).

**Fall:** Generator demand spikes before winter in northern markets. Holiday lighting installation searches appear (if you offer this service). Pre-winter electrical inspection searches increase.

**Winter:** Emergency electrical calls from storm damage and power outages. Indoor renovation electrical work increases. Smart home and lighting upgrade interest peaks around the holidays.

## Landing Page Optimization

With electrical CPCs averaging $10 to $30, you have more margin for testing than higher-CPC trades. Use this advantage to test landing page variations.

### A/B Testing Priorities
1. Phone number placement and size
2. Form length (3 fields vs 5 fields)
3. Hero image (technician at work vs completed installation)
4. Trust signal selection (license number vs review count vs years in business)
5. CTA button text ("Get Free Estimate" vs "Call Now" vs "Schedule Service")

### Conversion Rate Benchmarks
- Emergency landing pages: 15 to 25% conversion rate
- Panel upgrade landing pages: 8 to 15% conversion rate
- EV charger landing pages: 10 to 18% conversion rate
- Generator landing pages: 8 to 12% conversion rate
- General electrician landing pages: 6 to 10% conversion rate

If your pages fall below these benchmarks, prioritize landing page optimization before increasing ad spend. Doubling your conversion rate has the same effect as doubling your budget at half the cost.
