# Local SEO for Remodeling Companies

Remodeling is a high-consideration purchase. A homeowner planning a kitchen renovation is about to spend $25,000 to $80,000 and live in a construction zone for 6 to 12 weeks. They are not going to call the first company they see on Google. They are going to research extensively, compare portfolios, read reviews carefully, and narrow their list to two or three companies before requesting consultations.

This extended research phase means remodeling local SEO is not about capturing impulse decisions. It is about being visible and impressive at every stage of the homeowner's research journey. When they first search "kitchen remodel ideas," your design inspiration content should appear. When they search "kitchen remodeling companies near me," your Map Pack listing should dominate. When they search "ABC Remodeling reviews," your 4.8-star Google profile should reassure them.

Local SEO for remodeling companies requires deep content across service categories, a portfolio that rivals design magazines, and a review profile that demonstrates consistent quality on high-stakes projects.

## Keyword Strategy by Project Type

Remodeling keywords separate by room, project type, and research stage. Your content must target all three dimensions.

### Kitchen Remodeling Keywords

Kitchen remodels represent the highest-ticket, highest-volume category in residential remodeling.

**Decision-stage keywords (ready to hire):**
- "Kitchen remodeling near me"
- "Kitchen remodeling company [city]"
- "Kitchen renovation contractor"
- "Kitchen remodel cost [city]"

**Research-stage keywords (exploring options):**
- "Kitchen remodel ideas"
- "Kitchen remodel cost"
- "How long does a kitchen remodel take"
- "Kitchen remodel before and after"
- "Modern kitchen design"

**Specific-feature keywords:**
- "Kitchen cabinet replacement"
- "Kitchen countertop installation"
- "Kitchen island remodel"
- "Open concept kitchen renovation"
- "Kitchen flooring installation"

Each keyword cluster needs dedicated content. Research-stage keywords belong in blog posts and inspiration galleries. Decision-stage keywords belong on your main kitchen remodeling service page. Specific-feature keywords warrant individual service pages or detailed sections within the main page.

### Bathroom Remodeling Keywords

Bathroom remodels are the second-highest volume category with average ticket values of $10,000 to $40,000.

**Decision-stage keywords:**
- "Bathroom remodeling near me"
- "Bathroom renovation contractor [city]"
- "Bathroom remodel cost"
- "Master bathroom remodel"

**Research-stage keywords:**
- "Bathroom remodel ideas"
- "Small bathroom renovation"
- "Walk-in shower conversion"
- "Bathroom vanity ideas"

**Specific-feature keywords:**
- "Walk-in shower installation"
- "Tub to shower conversion"
- "Bathroom tile installation"
- "Freestanding tub installation"
- "Heated floor installation bathroom"

### Whole-Home and Addition Keywords

**Keywords:**
- "Home addition contractor"
- "Room addition cost"
- "Whole home renovation"
- "Second story addition"
- "Garage conversion contractor"
- "Basement finishing"
- "ADU construction" (accessory dwelling unit)

These keywords generate the highest ticket values ($50,000 to $200,000+) but the lowest search volumes. They warrant dedicated pages but lower budget priority for paid promotion.

## Google Business Profile for Remodeling Companies

### Category Strategy

**Primary category:** General Contractor (or Remodeler if available in your market)

**Additional categories:**
- Kitchen Remodeler
- Bathroom Remodeler
- Home Builder (if you do additions)
- Flooring Contractor
- Cabinet Maker (if you build custom cabinets)

### Portfolio Posts

GBP posts for remodeling companies should showcase completed projects with multiple photos. Post three to four times per week with:

- Before-and-after photo pairs from recently completed projects
- Design detail shots (custom tile work, cabinetry, lighting fixtures)
- In-progress construction photos (framing, rough plumbing, electrical)
- Material highlights (specific countertop slabs, cabinet finishes, tile patterns)

Each post should mention the neighborhood, the project scope, and specific materials. "Just completed a kitchen renovation in [Neighborhood]. Custom maple shaker cabinets in Benjamin Moore 'Hale Navy,' Cambria Ironsbridge quartz countertops, and Waterworks tile backsplash."

### Review Strategy for Remodelers

Remodeling projects last weeks or months. The relationship between homeowner and contractor deepens over the course of the project. By the time the project is complete, you have spent more time with the homeowner than any other service provider in their life that year.

**Request timing:** One week after project completion. Give the homeowner time to live in their new space and appreciate the result. A review written while they are still excited about their new kitchen is more detailed and enthusiastic than one requested the day of the final walkthrough.

**Request approach:** Send a personal message from the project lead or company owner. "Hi [Name], we loved working on your kitchen. The Hale Navy cabinets came out beautifully. If you have a few minutes, a Google review sharing your experience would mean the world to us." Personalization from someone they worked with daily converts at a significantly higher rate than a generic automated request.

**Review content coaching:** Suggest that customers mention specific aspects of the project. "If you could mention the design process, the construction experience, and how the finished result turned out, that gives future customers a complete picture." Reviews that describe the full remodeling experience are more persuasive than generic "great company" reviews and contain keywords that support SEO.

## Project Portfolio as SEO Engine

Your portfolio is both a conversion tool and an SEO asset. Each detailed project showcase creates a unique indexed page targeting local and material-specific keywords.

### Portfolio Page Structure

**Project overview:** Brief summary of the scope. "Full kitchen renovation in Eagle Creek, Tampa. Complete gut and rebuild including new cabinets, countertops, flooring, lighting, plumbing fixtures, and appliances."

**Before-and-after gallery:** 10 to 15 photos per project including wide shots, detail shots, and the transformation comparison.

**Design choices:** Specific materials, finishes, and brands used. "Cabinets: Custom maple shaker in Benjamin Moore Hale Navy HC-154. Countertops: Cambria Ironsbridge quartz. Backsplash: Waterworks Cafe 3x6 subway tile in White. Flooring: European white oak hardwood. Lighting: Visual Comfort pendants over island."

**Project specifications:** Timeline, approximate square footage, and budget range (general: "investment range $45,000 to $65,000").

**Homeowner testimonial:** A quote from the customer about their experience with the design process and the finished result.

**Neighborhood context:** Brief mention of the area and any architectural considerations. "This 1985 colonial in Eagle Creek had the original builder-grade kitchen with laminate countertops and wire-rack cabinets."

### SEO Value of Portfolio Pages

Each project page ranks for:
- Neighborhood + remodeling keywords ("Eagle Creek kitchen remodel")
- Material keywords ("Cambria Ironsbridge countertop," "Hale Navy kitchen cabinets")
- Style keywords ("modern farmhouse kitchen Tampa")
- Project type keywords ("kitchen gut renovation before and after")

A remodeling company that publishes 3 to 4 project showcases per month creates 36 to 48 new SEO-rich pages per year. After three years, the portfolio alone generates hundreds of keyword rankings and significant organic traffic.

## Local Area Pages

Remodeling area pages should reference:

**Home ages and common renovation needs.** "Homes in [neighborhood] were built in the early 2000s. Original builder kitchens typically feature 42-inch oak cabinets, laminate countertops, and basic ceramic tile backsplash. Most homeowners in this community upgrade to painted or stained hardwood cabinets, quartz countertops, and designer tile within 15 to 20 years of construction."

**HOA and permit considerations.** "Renovations in [community] require HOA Architectural Review Board approval before beginning. We handle the submission process and ensure all exterior modifications comply with community guidelines."

**Completed projects in the area.** "We have completed 12 kitchen and bathroom renovations in [neighborhood] since 2020." Link to the relevant project showcases.

## Competing with Big Remodeling Companies

The remodeling industry includes large companies with massive marketing budgets (Bath Fitter, Re-Bath, N-Hance, Kitchen Tune-Up). These franchises rank well for generic terms but typically offer limited service ranges.

**Your advantages:**
- Custom work vs franchise templates
- Full-service renovation vs single-product solutions
- Local reputation with neighborhood-specific experience
- Portfolio depth showing diverse styles and scopes
- Personal relationship with company owner and project leads

Build content that positions the value of custom, full-service remodeling versus the cookie-cutter franchise approach. A homeowner comparing a $15,000 Bath Fitter bathroom to a $25,000 custom renovation needs to understand what they gain (and lose) at each price point.

Track monthly:
- Map Pack rankings for kitchen and bathroom remodeling keywords
- Organic traffic to portfolio pages and inspiration content
- Consultation request volume by source
- Review velocity and detail quality
- Portfolio page indexing and ranking progress
- Time on site and pages per session (remodeling visitors spend more time researching)
