### Service-Specific Structure

Each service category gets its own campaign with dedicated keywords, ad copy, and landing pages.

An HVAC company in Clearwater runs separate campaigns for:
- AC repair (highest urgency, highest conversion rate)
- AC installation/replacement (higher ticket, longer sales cycle)
- Heating repair (low volume but spikes during cold fronts)
- Maintenance/tune-ups (lower CPC, good for filling capacity)

This separation lets us allocate budget where it produces the best return. During summer, AC repair gets maximum budget. During shoulder seasons, maintenance and installation campaigns take priority.

### Landing Pages

Every ad points to a landing page built for that service in Clearwater. ## Seasonal Budget Strategy

Clearwater's seasonal patterns directly affect Google Ads performance and should drive budget allocation.

**Peak Summer (June through September):** HVAC search volume hits maximum. Increase AC repair and installation budgets by 30 to 50 percent. CPCs rise with competition, but conversion rates also increase because of urgency.

**Tourist Season (November through April):** Property management searches increase. Vacation rental maintenance keywords become active. Adjust campaigns to capture commercial service opportunities alongside residential.

**Hurricane Season (June through November):** Standing campaigns for roofing, water damage, and emergency services ready to scale when storms threaten. Pre-storm preparation campaigns run proactively.

**Shoulder Seasons:** Focus budgets on planned services: maintenance contracts, system upgrades, remodeling projects. CPCs drop as urgency decreases, making these campaigns efficient for building long-term customer relationships.
