# TV & Streaming Advertising in Orlando, FL

Orlando is the 17th largest television market in America. The DMA reaches 1.8 million households. For a home service business looking to build brand dominance in Central Florida, television and streaming advertising offer a scale of reach that no other medium can match.

The contractors winning market share in Orlando right now are not the ones spending the most on Google Ads. They are the ones who show up on screens across Orange County every day. They are the names homeowners remember when a pipe bursts or an AC unit fails. TV builds that kind of recall in ways that search ads and social media posts cannot.

## The Orlando TV Market

The Orlando-Daytona Beach-Melbourne DMA extends well beyond the Orlando metro into Volusia, Brevard, and other counties. That broad footprint is irrelevant for a home service business focused on Orange County. What matters is reaching Orlando homeowners specifically.

Spectrum Reach provides zone-based cable advertising within the Orlando DMA. Your commercial airs in Orange County zones covering Winter Park, Dr. Phillips, Lake Nona, and the communities where your customers live. No wasted impressions in Daytona Beach or Melbourne.

Connected TV tightens this targeting further. Streaming ads reach Orlando households at the zip code level across Hulu, Peacock, YouTube TV, Tubi, and other platforms. A company serving only the southwest Orlando corridor can run ads exclusively in the zip codes covering Dr. Phillips, Windermere, and Horizon West.

## Why TV Advertising Works in Orlando

### Breaking Through the Noise

Orlando's home service market is overwhelmed with advertising. Google Ads, social media campaigns, door hangers, vehicle wraps, and lead generation platforms all compete for homeowner attention. Most of that advertising is easily ignored or scrolled past.

Television advertising commands attention differently. A 30-second commercial on a screen in someone's living room gets watched. Connected TV ads on streaming platforms cannot be skipped on most placements. Your message reaches the viewer in full, with the visual and audio impact that builds memory.

In a market where every competitor runs Google Ads, TV advertising differentiates your brand. The homeowner who sees your commercial twice a week develops familiarity that translates into preference when they need a contractor.

### Orlando's Growth Means New Customers

Orlando adds roughly 1,000 new residents every week. These newcomers have no established relationships with local contractors. They do not know who to call when the AC breaks or a pipe leaks. TV advertising introduces your company to these new arrivals before they ever face a home service emergency.

A newcomer who moves to Lake Nona and sees your commercial during their first week in Orlando will remember your name when they need a plumber three months later. That brand recall is nearly impossible to build through search advertising alone, which only captures people actively looking for a service.

### Reaching the Vacation Rental Industry

Orlando's vacation rental market is massive. Thousands of properties near Walt Disney World, Universal Studios, and the Convention Center serve the 75 million annual visitors to the Orlando area. Property managers who maintain these rentals watch local television and streaming content. TV advertising positions your company as the established, reputable contractor they want managing their property maintenance.

A commercial that positions your company as a property management partner reaches decision-makers who control budgets for dozens or hundreds of properties. One property management relationship can generate more annual revenue than hundreds of individual homeowner jobs.

## Building an Orlando TV Campaign

### Creative Production

Orlando's competitive market demands professional creative. Your commercial should feature:

**Real Orlando locations.** Film your team working in a recognizable Orlando neighborhood. A roofing crew in Windermere. An HVAC technician servicing a home with the Lake Nona Town Center in the background. A plumber pulling up to a classic Winter Park bungalow. These visual cues tell Orlando viewers you are local.

**Your actual team.** The people who will show up at the homeowner's door. Uniforms, name badges, company trucks. Homeowners in Orlando are cautious about who they let into their homes. Seeing your real people on television builds the trust that overcomes that caution.

**A compelling message.** Lead with the homeowner's problem. "When your AC fails in August." "When a pipe bursts at 2 AM." Then present your company as the solution. Keep it simple. One message per commercial.

**A clear call to action.** Phone number displayed for at least five seconds. Website URL. "Call now for same-day service." Make the next step obvious.

### Media Planning for Orlando

Network and platform selection depends on your target customer:

- **HGTV and home improvement networks** reach homeowners actively engaged with their properties
- **Local news (WESH 2, WFTV 9, News 13)** reaches older demographics with high homeownership and property values
- **ESPN and sports programming** provides broad reach during UCF, Magic, and Orlando City games
- **Streaming platforms** capture younger demographics who have cut cable but watch hours of content daily
- **Telemundo and Univision** reach Orlando's large Hispanic community, a significant and underserved market

We allocate impressions across networks and dayparts based on your trade, target customer profile, and budget.

### Seasonal Strategy

Orlando's year-round warm climate means HVAC advertising is effective twelve months a year, with summer peaks. Roofing campaigns ramp up during hurricane season. Plumbing and electrical maintain steady demand.

For vacation rental focused campaigns, we increase weight during Orlando's peak tourist seasons: summer, Thanksgiving, Christmas, and spring break. Property managers are most receptive to new contractor relationships during their busiest periods.

## Measurement and Attribution

We measure Orlando TV campaigns through:

- **Branded search lift:** Increases in Google searches for your company name correlating with TV flight schedules

- **Call tracking:** Dedicated phone numbers for TV campaigns to measure direct response

Monthly reporting shows the concrete impact of TV advertising on your overall marketing performance and lead generation.
