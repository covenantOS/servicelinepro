## Campaign Structure for St. Pete

### Geographic Precision

St. Petersburg's peninsula geography lets us build exceptionally tight targeting. We configure campaigns to cover the zip codes you actually serve. A plumber based near Tyrone might target 33710, 33712, 33713, 33714, and 33715 while excluding the beach communities if drive times make those jobs impractical.

For companies serving the wider Pinellas County market, we build separate campaigns for St. Petersburg proper, the beach communities (Treasure Island, Madeira Beach, St. Pete Beach), and northern cities (Largo, Seminole, Clearwater). Each campaign gets its own budget, keywords, and ad copy tailored to that geography.

### Service-Specific Campaigns

We organize campaigns by service category. An HVAC company in St. Pete gets separate campaigns for AC repair, AC installation, heating repair, and maintenance. Each campaign targets keywords specific to that service, with ad copy that matches the search intent.

This structure prevents budget cannibalization. If AC repair generates a $35 cost per lead while AC installation runs $90, we allocate budget where it performs best without one service stealing impressions from another.

### Landing Pages That Convert

Every ad points to a dedicated landing page for that service in St. Petersburg. When someone searches "AC repair St. Petersburg," they land on a page about AC repair in St. Petersburg, not your homepage. ### Seasonal Adjustments

St. Pete's seasonal patterns mirror greater Tampa Bay but with some local nuances. The beach communities see population swings with tourist seasons. The condo-heavy areas around downtown experience different demand patterns than single-family neighborhoods. We adjust bids, budgets, and ad copy throughout the year to match these patterns.

Summer means maximum HVAC budgets. Hurricane season means standing roofing and restoration campaigns ready to scale. Winter means heating searches during cold fronts and seasonal maintenance campaigns targeting snowbirds who just arrived and need their systems checked.
