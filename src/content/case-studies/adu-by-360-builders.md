## Challenge

ADU By 360 Builders is a full-service construction firm in Los Angeles specializing in Accessory Dwelling Units. California's ADU boom created a surge of homeowners exploring backyard homes for rental income, aging parents, or adult children who cannot afford LA housing costs. The market opportunity was massive.

360 Builders was driving traffic to their website through Google search and paid advertising. The visitors were there. But conversions were not following. The site averaged a conversion rate below 3%, which meant for every 100 visitors who came looking for ADU construction information, 97 left without making contact.

The root cause was a timing mismatch. Los Angeles homeowners researching ADU projects do much of their browsing during evenings and weekends. They are scrolling through contractor websites after dinner, comparing options while watching TV, or doing research on Saturday mornings. But 360 Builders' team was only available during standard business hours.

A homeowner who visits the site at 8:30 PM with a specific question about ADU permitting in their neighborhood has zero patience for a contact form that promises a response "within 24-48 hours." By morning, they have already filled out forms on three competitor sites and scheduled consultations with whoever responded first.

The ADU market in Los Angeles is also complex. Homeowners have questions about zoning, setback requirements, garage conversions versus new builds, financing options, and realistic project timelines. These are not impulse purchases. They are six-figure construction projects that require education before commitment.

## Strategy

ServiceLine Pro deployed an AI ChatBot specifically designed for ADU construction conversations, turning the 360 Builders website from a static brochure into a 24/7 sales and education tool.

The ChatBot was trained on Los Angeles ADU regulations, common project types, typical timelines, and realistic pricing ranges. When a homeowner asks "Can I build an ADU on my property in Eagle Rock?" the bot can discuss general zoning eligibility, explain the permitting process, and describe what a typical project in that area looks like, all within seconds.

The automated scheduling component was equally critical. Once the chat qualifies a visitor as a serious prospect, it offers to book a free consultation directly on the builder's calendar. No phone tag. No waiting for a callback. The homeowner goes from curiosity to a confirmed appointment in a single conversation, regardless of the time of day.

We structured the chat flows around the actual decision journey of an ADU buyer. Early-stage visitors get educational content: what is an ADU, what does the process look like, what are the costs. Later-stage visitors who already know what they want get fast-tracked to scheduling. The bot adapts based on the questions being asked.

Landing pages were optimized to support the chat experience. Each page includes project photos of completed ADUs, specific cost ranges for different project types (garage conversion, detached new build, attached addition), and answers to the top questions homeowners ask before their first consultation.

## Results

The AI ChatBot transformed 360 Builders' lead generation:

- **750% return on investment**, approximately 10x the construction industry standard for digital marketing spend
- **17 qualified leads generated from just 137 visitors**, a conversion rate of 12.4%
- **24/7 automated scheduling** eliminated the response time gap that was costing the company leads during evenings and weekends
- **63% of leads originated from after-hours chat conversations**, confirming that the timing mismatch was the primary conversion barrier
- **Average time from first visit to booked consultation dropped from 3.2 days to 4 hours**
- **Lead quality improved** because the chat pre-qualified homeowners on budget, timeline, and project scope before the consultation

The 12.4% conversion rate is remarkable for construction. The industry average hovers around 2-3%. One in eight visitors became a qualified lead, and most of those leads already had a consultation booked before a human team member ever got involved.

## Conclusion

ADU By 360 Builders was not failing at marketing. They were failing at availability. The leads existed. The traffic was there. They just needed someone, or something, to answer the door when prospects knocked. The AI ChatBot became that around-the-clock presence, turning a 3% conversion rate into 12.4% and delivering 750% ROI. In the LA ADU market, the builder who responds first wins the project. 360 Builders now responds first every time.
