## Challenge

Towpath Credit Union in Ohio faced a challenge that every financial institution knows well: the gap between loan approval and loan closing. Members would get approved for auto loans, home equity lines, or personal loans, and then disappear. They would not return calls. They would miss deadlines. They would let approvals expire and take their business to a competitor who happened to reach them at the right moment.

The problem was not the loan products or the rates. Towpath's offerings were competitive. The problem was communication. The credit union relied on phone calls and emails to move members through the loan process, and both channels had deteriorating engagement rates. Members screened calls from unknown numbers. Emails sat unread in crowded inboxes. Critical loan updates, document requests, and approval notifications were lost in the noise.

This was not a minor inefficiency. Every lost loan closing represented thousands of dollars in unrealized interest income. Multiply that across hundreds of loan applications per month, and the revenue impact was significant. Towpath's loan officers were spending hours chasing members by phone, leaving voicemails that were never returned, and watching approved loans go stale.

The credit union also needed any communication solution to meet strict financial industry compliance requirements. Member data, loan details, and financial information could not flow through unsecured channels. Whatever replaced the phone-and-email approach had to be both effective and auditable.

## Strategy

ServiceLine implemented a secure, compliant SMS communication system designed specifically for the lending workflow. The system integrated with Towpath's loan processing pipeline to deliver automated text messages at critical stages of the loan lifecycle.

When a member applied for a loan, they received SMS updates at each stage: application received, documents needed, approval notification, closing scheduled, and final confirmation. Each message was actionable, containing direct links or clear instructions for the member's next step.

The approach recognized a fundamental behavioral reality: people read text messages. SMS open rates exceed 95%, compared to roughly 20% for email and single-digit answer rates for calls from business numbers. By meeting members on the channel they actually use, Towpath eliminated the communication gap that was killing loan closings.

For document collection, the system sent secure links where members could upload required paperwork directly from their phone. No printing. No scanning. No driving to a branch. A member could photograph their pay stub and upload it in under a minute.

The loan officers' workflow changed dramatically. Instead of spending their mornings making outbound calls and leaving voicemails, they reviewed a dashboard of member interactions. They could see who had opened messages, who had completed actions, and who needed a personal follow-up. Their time shifted from chasing to closing.

ServiceLine ensured every aspect of the system met financial industry compliance standards. Messages were encrypted. Member data was protected. Audit trails were maintained for every communication. The credit union's compliance team signed off on the implementation before launch.

## Results

The impact on Towpath's lending operation was immediate and measurable:

- **10-15% increase in loan-closing ratio**, converting approved applications into funded loans at a significantly higher rate
- **#1 in loan growth** among their peer group of similarly sized credit unions
- **Improved member experience** with faster, more transparent communication throughout the loan process
- **Reduced loan officer workload** as automated updates replaced manual phone follow-ups
- **Full compliance** maintained across all SMS communications

Becoming number one in loan growth among their peer group is a competitive achievement that reflects both the volume of new loans and the improved conversion of approvals to closings.

## Conclusion

Towpath Credit Union was losing funded loans to a communication problem, not a product problem. Members wanted the loans. They just were not reachable through phone calls and emails. ServiceLine's secure SMS system closed the gap by meeting members on the one channel they consistently check. A 10-15% improvement in loan-closing ratio and the top spot in peer group loan growth demonstrate that in financial services, the institution that communicates most effectively wins the business. Towpath stopped calling into the void and started texting into results.
