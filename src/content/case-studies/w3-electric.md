## Challenge

W3 Electric is a licensed electrical contractor serving the Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex. They handle everything from residential panel upgrades to commercial electrical installations. Their technicians are skilled, their reviews are strong, and their reputation in the field is solid.

But their website was killing their lead flow.

The existing site looked professional enough at first glance. But it failed at the one job a service business website has: converting visitors into leads. The contact form was buried on a subpage. The phone number was small and not clickable on mobile. There was no way for a visitor to get an immediate answer to a question without calling during business hours. Service descriptions were vague. There was no clear call to action on any page.

Analytics told the story clearly. The site received decent traffic from their existing Google rankings and referral sources, but the conversion rate was below 1%. For every 100 people who visited the site, fewer than one picked up the phone or filled out a form. Visitors were arriving, looking around, and leaving to call a competitor with a better website.

W3 Electric wasn't losing because of poor service or bad pricing. They were losing because their website didn't give visitors a reason to take the next step.

## Strategy

We designed and built a new website from the ground up with a single objective: convert every possible visitor into a lead.

The redesign started with structure. Every page followed a conversion-focused layout: clear headline stating the service, specific description of what the customer gets, trust signals (license numbers, insurance, years in business, review ratings), and a prominent call to action above the fold. The phone number was large, clickable, and visible on every page. The contact form was simplified to three fields and placed on every service page.

We built dedicated landing pages for each of their core services: residential electrical repair, panel upgrades, EV charger installation, commercial wiring, lighting installation, and emergency electrical service. Each page targeted specific keywords and addressed the exact questions a Dallas homeowner or business owner would have about that service.

The AI chat integration was the differentiator. We added an intelligent chat widget that engages visitors within seconds of landing on the site. The chat handles common questions about services, pricing ranges, availability, and service areas. It qualifies leads by asking about the type of work needed, the property type, and the timeline. Qualified leads are routed directly to W3 Electric's scheduling system or flagged for immediate callback.

The chat operates 24/7. Someone searching for an electrician at 11 PM after a breaker trips can describe their problem and get a response immediately. Before the redesign, that visitor would have bounced. Now they become a lead.

We also optimized page load speed, implemented mobile-first design (over 70% of their traffic comes from phones), and added schema markup for local business and service data.

## Results

The new website launched and the impact was immediate:

- **1,400% return on investment** within the first 90 days
- **33 new leads generated** through the website in the first three months
- **Conversion rate increased from under 1% to 4.8%** across all pages
- **AI chat captured 19 of the 33 leads**, with 11 of those coming outside business hours
- **Average time to first engagement dropped from never to 8 seconds** with the chat widget
- **Mobile conversion rate tripled** with the responsive redesign

The 1,400% ROI calculation is straightforward. The cost of the website redesign and chat integration was a fixed investment. The 33 leads generated in 90 days, with W3 Electric's average job value and close rate, produced revenue that exceeded the investment by 14 times.

The AI chat data was particularly revealing. More than half of all leads came through chat rather than phone or form. And a third of those chat leads came outside of business hours, when no human was available to answer. Without the chat, those 11 leads would have been lost entirely.

## Conclusion

W3 Electric had the traffic. They had the reputation. They had the Google rankings. What they didn't have was a website that closed the deal. The redesign wasn't about making things prettier. It was about removing every barrier between a visitor and a phone call. The AI chat turned their website from a digital brochure into a 24/7 lead generation machine. Thirty-three new leads and 1,400% ROI in 90 days. That's what happens when your website actually does its job.
