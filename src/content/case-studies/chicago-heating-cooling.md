## Challenge

Chicago Heating & Cooling had been serving the Chicagoland area for over 15 years. Their technicians were skilled. Their customer satisfaction scores were high. But none of that mattered when homeowners searched "HVAC repair near me" and found three competitors before them.

The Chicago HVAC market is one of the most saturated in the Midwest. Over 400 licensed HVAC contractors serve the metro area, and the largest players spend tens of thousands per month on Google Ads. Chicago Heating & Cooling was invisible in organic search results, buried below page one in the local map pack, and generating almost zero inbound leads from Google.

Their previous marketing consisted of a basic website built in 2016 and a Google Business Profile that hadn't been updated in two years. No review strategy. No citation consistency. No local content targeting the dozens of suburbs they served.

## Strategy

We built a local SEO campaign around three pillars: Google Business Profile optimization, review generation, and hyperlocal content.

First, we overhauled their Google Business Profile. We rewrote every section with keyword-rich descriptions targeting "furnace repair Chicago," "AC installation Chicago," and 30+ similar high-intent phrases. We added service area pages for each suburb they covered, uploaded fresh photos of their fleet and team monthly, and published Google Posts weekly highlighting seasonal promotions.

Second, we implemented a review generation system. After every completed service call, customers received an automated text message with a direct link to leave a Google review. We coached the team on how to ask for reviews naturally during the close of each appointment. The goal was simple: outpace competitors in both review volume and recency.

Third, we built location-specific landing pages for each of their 12 primary service areas. Each page contained unique content about that community's common HVAC issues, local weather patterns affecting heating and cooling demand, and specific service offerings. This wasn't template content with the city name swapped out. Each page was written from scratch.

## Results

Within eight months, Chicago Heating & Cooling saw measurable, revenue-impacting results:

- **20% increase in total revenue** compared to the same period the previous year
- **60+ new Google reviews** with a 4.8 average star rating
- **Top 3 map pack rankings** for 18 primary HVAC keywords across Chicago and surrounding suburbs
- **47% increase in website traffic** from organic search
- **32% increase in phone calls** directly from Google Business Profile

Their Google Business Profile went from averaging 1,200 views per month to over 4,500. The phone started ringing with customers who found them through organic search rather than paid ads.

"We used to spend $3,000 a month on Google Ads and wonder where the leads were going," said the owner. "Now organic search drives more calls than paid ever did, and we cut our ad spend in half."

## Conclusion

Chicago Heating & Cooling proved that even in a hypercompetitive metro market, a focused local SEO strategy beats throwing money at ads. The combination of a fully optimized Google Business Profile, a disciplined review generation process, and genuinely useful local content created a compounding advantage that continues to grow. Their competitors are still buying clicks. Chicago Heating & Cooling owns their rankings.
