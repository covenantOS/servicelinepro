## Challenge

Wizcrete Concrete Solutions is an award-winning concrete resurfacing company based in Australia. They had earned industry recognition for their craftsmanship and built a strong portfolio of residential and commercial projects. Their reputation in the field was not the problem.

The problem was managing the gap between website traffic and actual conversations. Wizcrete was driving visitors to their site through organic search and referrals, but the inquiry process was slow and manual. A visitor would fill out a contact form. Someone at Wizcrete would see it hours later. They would call or email back. By then, the homeowner had already contacted two or three competitors.

In concrete resurfacing, speed matters more than most contractors realize. A homeowner looking to resurface their driveway or pool deck is typically comparing three to five companies simultaneously. The first company to respond with helpful information wins the job more often than the company with the best portfolio.

Wizcrete's team was spending too much time on repetitive back-and-forth: answering the same questions about pricing ranges, service areas, timelines, and preparation requirements. These conversations were necessary but predictable. They consumed hours each week that could have been spent on project management and client relationships.

The website itself was visually strong, showcasing their work effectively. But it operated like a digital brochure rather than a sales tool. Visitors could look, but they could not interact.

## Strategy

ServiceLine Pro deployed an AI ChatBot as the primary engagement tool on Wizcrete's website, supported by broader conversion optimization across the site.

The ChatBot was trained on Wizcrete's specific services, pricing frameworks, service areas, and frequently asked questions. When a visitor lands on the site and asks "How much does driveway resurfacing cost?" the bot provides a realistic range based on typical project sizes, then asks qualifying questions: What is the approximate area? What is the current surface condition? When are you looking to have the work done?

This instant engagement replaced the old form-and-wait process entirely. A visitor could go from first question to qualified lead in under two minutes, at any hour of the day.

We optimized the chat flow to mirror the actual sales conversation Wizcrete's team has on the phone. The bot collects the project details, sets realistic expectations, and schedules a site visit. By the time a Wizcrete team member follows up, they already have a complete picture of the project and a committed appointment.

Beyond the chat, we restructured key landing pages to reduce friction. Portfolio images were reorganized by project type so visitors could quickly find work similar to their own project. Calls to action were simplified. The number of clicks between landing and inquiry dropped from four to one.

We also implemented analytics to track the full visitor journey from first page view through chat engagement to lead capture, giving Wizcrete clear visibility into which traffic sources and which pages drove the highest-quality inquiries.

## Results

The AI-powered approach delivered strong, measurable outcomes:

- **687% return on investment** on the total project cost
- **26 qualified leads generated from just 287 visitors**, a conversion rate of 9.1%, well above the construction industry average of 2-3%
- **Streamlined communication** reduced the time spent on initial lead qualification by approximately 10 hours per week
- **Average response time dropped from 4+ hours to under 15 seconds**, putting Wizcrete first in the conversation with nearly every prospect
- **Lead quality increased** because the chat pre-qualified visitors before they ever reached the sales team

The 9.1% conversion rate is the standout metric. In an industry where most websites convert at 2-3%, nearly one in ten Wizcrete visitors became a qualified lead. That efficiency compounds over time as traffic grows.

## Conclusion

Wizcrete Concrete Solutions proved that award-winning work deserves an award-winning sales process. Their craftsmanship was never in question. What changed was how quickly and effectively they connected with people who wanted that craftsmanship. The AI ChatBot did not replace their team. It gave their team a head start on every conversation, and that head start translated to 687% ROI.
