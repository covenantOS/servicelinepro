## Challenge

Bi-State Pest Control served communities across the Missouri-Illinois border region for over a decade. They had a loyal customer base built on referrals and repeat service. But their Google presence told a different story.

When potential customers searched for pest control in their area, Bi-State didn't show up. Their Google Business Profile had minimal information, inconsistent business hours across directories, and fewer than 10 reviews. Competitors with weaker service but stronger digital presence were capturing every searcher who didn't already know Bi-State by name.

Their website ranked for almost nothing. Their citation profile was a mess, with different phone numbers and addresses scattered across dozens of directories. Google had no confidence in their business data, and it showed in their rankings.

## Strategy

We launched an aggressive local SEO campaign targeting three core areas: Google Business Profile optimization, citation cleanup and building, and a structured review generation program.

The GBP overhaul came first. We rewrote the business description to target high-volume pest control keywords specific to their service area. We added every relevant service category Google offers for pest control companies. We uploaded professional photos, created a posting schedule of twice-weekly Google Posts, and built out the Q&A section with answers to the 20 most common pest control questions in their region.

Citation building and cleanup ran in parallel. We audited every major directory, identified 47 listings with incorrect or inconsistent information, and corrected each one manually. Then we built new citations on 85+ authoritative local and industry-specific directories. Every listing matched their GBP data exactly: same name, same address, same phone number, same categories.

For review generation, we implemented an automated follow-up system triggered after each service appointment. Customers received a text within two hours of service completion with a direct link to their Google review page. The team was trained to mention reviews during service calls without being pushy.

## Results

The numbers moved fast and they moved big:

- **2,784% increase in Google search appearances** within the first six months
- **357% increase in Google Business Profile views** from search and maps combined
- **120% increase in phone calls** directly attributed to their Google Business Profile
- **85+ new Google reviews** earned in the first year, pushing their average to 4.9 stars
- **Top 3 map pack placement** for "pest control near me" and 14 related keywords across their service area

The search appearance number bears repeating: 2,784%. That means for every one time Google showed their listing before the campaign, it now showed them nearly 28 times. That's not incremental improvement. That's a complete transformation of their digital visibility.

Phone calls more than doubled, and each of those calls came from someone actively searching for pest control. These weren't cold leads. They were homeowners with bugs in their kitchen ready to book service that day.

## Conclusion

Bi-State Pest Control had the service quality and the customer satisfaction to compete with anyone in their market. What they lacked was visibility. A disciplined local SEO strategy built on clean data, consistent citations, and genuine customer reviews turned them from invisible to dominant. The referrals still come in. But now Google sends customers too, and it sends a lot of them.
