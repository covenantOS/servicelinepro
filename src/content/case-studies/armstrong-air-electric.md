## Challenge

Armstrong Air & Electric had built a solid reputation in the Orlando, FL market for HVAC and electrical services. Their technicians were skilled. Their reviews were strong. But their website told none of that story.

The existing site looked like it was built in 2014 and never touched again. Load times dragged past five seconds. The contact form buried three clicks deep. Mobile visitors, who made up 68% of their traffic, hit a layout so broken most bounced within ten seconds.

For a company competing against national franchises with seven-figure marketing budgets, every lost visitor was a lost job. Orlando homeowners searching "AC repair near me" at 2 PM on a July afternoon are not browsing. They are buying. Armstrong needed a website that matched the urgency of their customers.

The core problem was simple: traffic existed, but conversions did not. Their site generated fewer than 15 leads per month despite solid organic rankings and a growing Google Business Profile.

## Strategy

ServiceLine Pro rebuilt Armstrong's website from the ground up with one goal: turn visitors into booked appointments.

The redesign started with speed. We stripped unnecessary scripts, compressed images, and moved to a modern hosting architecture. Load time dropped below two seconds on mobile.

Next, we restructured the entire user journey. Every page now funnels toward a single clear action: request service. Phone numbers are sticky on mobile. The contact form lives above the fold on every service page. We eliminated the "About Us" rabbit hole that was bleeding visitors away from conversion points.

We added an AI-powered chat widget that engages visitors within three seconds of landing. The chat qualifies leads by asking about their service need, location, and timeline. It operates around the clock, which matters when Orlando AC units fail at midnight in August.

Every service page got rewritten with conversion-focused copy. Instead of generic descriptions of HVAC repair, we wrote pages that addressed specific pain points: "AC blowing warm air," "circuit breaker keeps tripping," "thermostat not responding." These pages matched the exact phrases homeowners type into Google.

We also implemented call tracking and form analytics so Armstrong could see exactly which pages and which traffic sources produced paying customers, not just clicks.

## Results

Within the first 90 days, Armstrong Air & Electric saw:

- **52 new qualified leads** generated directly through the redesigned website
- **160% return on investment** on the total project cost
- **3.2x increase in mobile conversion rate** compared to the previous site
- **Average time on site increased 47%**, indicating visitors were engaging with content rather than bouncing
- **AI chat handled 38% of all lead captures**, including nights and weekends when the office was closed

The AI chat proved especially valuable during off-hours. Over 40% of chat-generated leads came in between 6 PM and 8 AM, conversations that would have been lost entirely on the old site.

## Conclusion

Armstrong Air & Electric proved that a service company does not need a massive ad budget to compete with national brands. They needed a website that works as hard as their technicians do. The combination of fast load times, clear conversion paths, and AI-powered engagement turned an underperforming site into their strongest lead generation tool. Every dollar spent on the redesign returned $2.60 in new business within the first quarter.
