## Challenge

Leia's Loving Dog Care was a small, owner-operated pet services business offering dog walking, pet sitting, and overnight boarding. The owner had built a strong reputation through word-of-mouth in her neighborhood, but growth had plateaued.

The problem was straightforward: pet owners searching Google for dog care services in her area never found her. Her Google Business Profile was bare bones. It had a business name, an address, and nothing else. No photos of the dogs she cared for. No description of her services. No reviews from the dozens of happy clients who trusted her with their pets every week.

Competitors with flashy websites and dozens of reviews dominated the local map pack. Leia's Loving Dog Care was buried so deep in results that clicking "more businesses" three times still wouldn't surface it. For a business that depends on trust, being invisible on Google meant losing every potential customer who didn't hear about her through a friend.

## Strategy

We built a local SEO strategy tailored to a small pet services business with a limited budget. Every dollar and every hour had to count.

The Google Business Profile got a complete rebuild. We wrote a detailed business description targeting "dog walking," "pet sitting," and "dog boarding" keywords specific to her city and surrounding neighborhoods. We added all relevant service categories, uploaded 30+ photos of dogs in her care (with owner permission), and created a posting schedule featuring weekly updates about her services, pet care tips, and seasonal promotions.

We claimed and optimized listings across 40+ local directories and pet-specific platforms. Every listing was built with identical business information to establish trust signals with Google. We targeted directories that specifically served pet service businesses, including Rover, Yelp, and local chamber of commerce listings.

The review generation strategy was simple and personal. After each week of service, the owner sent a brief text to clients with a direct link to leave a Google review. Because she had genuine relationships with every client, the response rate was exceptionally high. Pet owners don't just leave reviews for good service. They leave reviews for people they trust with their animals.

## Results

Within five months, the numbers told a clear story:

- **1,169% increase in Google Business Profile views** from both search and maps
- **870% increase in search appearances** for pet service keywords in her area
- **100% increase in phone calls** directly from her Google Business Profile
- **35+ new Google reviews** with a perfect 5.0 star average
- **First page map pack rankings** for "dog walking near me," "pet sitter," and 8 related terms

The profile view growth was staggering for a business of this size. Going from a few hundred views per month to thousands meant that her business was now showing up every time a local pet owner searched for dog care. Each of those views represented someone in her service area actively looking for exactly what she offers.

The doubling of phone calls translated directly to new clients. Her schedule filled to capacity within four months, and she began maintaining a waitlist for the first time in her business's history.

## Conclusion

Leia's Loving Dog Care proves that local SEO works for businesses of every size. You don't need a massive budget or a corporate marketing team. You need a fully optimized Google Business Profile, consistent directory listings, and real reviews from real customers. The owner went from hoping for referrals to managing a waitlist. That's what happens when Google starts working for you instead of against you.
