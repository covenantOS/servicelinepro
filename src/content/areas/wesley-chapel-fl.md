## Wesley Chapel's Service Demand Profile

### HVAC in New Construction

Even new homes need HVAC service. Builder-grade systems, while functional, often benefit from upgrades and improved maintenance compared to what the builder's warranty covers. Filter changes, duct cleaning, thermostat upgrades, and efficiency optimization are all services Wesley Chapel homeowners need from their first year of ownership. As these homes age, the demand for repair and replacement grows.

### Plumbing for Modern Homes

Wesley Chapel's newer homes use PEX plumbing and modern fixtures, but they still experience issues. Hard water in Pasco County creates scaling and mineral buildup. Tankless water heaters need descaling. Garbage disposals and dishwashers need maintenance. And the occasional construction defect creates acute repair needs.

### Electrical Demand

Modern homes in Wesley Chapel are wired for today's demands, but homeowners continuously add load. Home offices, EV chargers, outdoor kitchen electrical, pool equipment, and smart home systems all create demand for electrical work beyond what the original construction provides.

### Roofing

Wesley Chapel's newer roofs have the advantage of current Florida building codes, which mandate superior wind resistance. But even modern shingle roofs take a beating from Florida's UV exposure, driving rain, and occasional severe weather. Inspection, maintenance, and repair services have steady demand even in a new construction market.

### Pest Control and Lawn Care

Florida's climate creates persistent demand for pest control and lawn maintenance. Wesley Chapel's new communities, built on formerly undeveloped land, often have particular challenges with ant colonies, termites, and wildlife intrusion. These service categories complement the core mechanical trades.
