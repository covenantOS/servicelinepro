## What Is Connected TV and Streaming Advertising?

Connected TV (CTV) refers to any television connected to the internet and used to stream content. Think Roku, Apple TV, Amazon Fire Stick, smart TVs with built-in apps, and gaming consoles. Streaming platforms like Hulu, Peacock, Paramount+, and hundreds of other ad-supported services deliver content to these devices.

Streaming TV advertising places your commercial within this content, the same way traditional TV commercials have worked for decades, but with one critical upgrade: precision targeting.

Traditional cable TV advertising forces you to buy broad time slots and hope your audience is watching. Streaming TV advertising lets you target specific households based on geography, demographics, interests, income level, and even homeownership status. You stop paying to reach apartment renters in zip codes you do not service. You start reaching homeowners in your exact service area who match your ideal customer profile.

The shift from linear TV to streaming is accelerating every quarter. Millions of households have cut the cord entirely. Millions more split their viewing between cable and streaming apps. If your advertising strategy ignores streaming platforms, you are missing a growing share of your potential customer base.

## How Streaming TV Advertising Builds Your Brand

Search marketing captures demand. Streaming TV advertising creates it.

When a homeowner sees your company's commercial on their smart TV three or four times over the course of a month, something shifts in their memory. Your company name moves from "never heard of them" to "I think I have seen them before." That familiarity is worth more than most contractors realize.

Research on advertising recall consistently shows that consumers are far more likely to choose a brand they recognize over one they are encountering for the first time. In home services, where trust is everything, brand recognition creates a powerful competitive moat.

### The Awareness-to-Booking Pipeline

Here is how streaming TV advertising translates into revenue for a home service business:

**Stage 1: Exposure.** A homeowner sees your 15 or 30-second commercial while streaming their favorite show. They are not actively looking for your service. But your name, logo, and message register.

**Stage 2: Reinforcement.** Over the following weeks, they see your ad again. Maybe twice more. Each impression strengthens the association between your brand and the service you provide.

**Stage 3: Trigger Event.** Their water heater fails. Their AC stops cooling. A circuit breaker keeps tripping. Now they need help.

**Stage 4: Recall and Action.** Instead of Googling "[plumber](/industries/plumbing/) near me" and clicking the first result, they search for your company by name. Or they skip Google entirely and call the number they remember from your commercial. Branded searches and direct calls are the highest-converting traffic a contractor can get.

**Stage 5: Compounding Returns.** As your brand awareness grows in your market, every other marketing channel performs better. Your Google Ads get higher click-through rates because people recognize your name. Your SEO rankings improve because more people search for you by name. Your close rate on estimates goes up because homeowners already feel like they know your company.

This compounding effect is why the largest home service companies in every market invest in brand advertising alongside performance marketing. Streaming TV advertising is the most cost-effective way for smaller contractors to access the same strategy.

## Precision Targeting for Home Service Markets

Generic reach is worthless. Showing your plumbing commercial to a college student in a dorm room does nothing for your business. Spectrum Reach's targeting capabilities let us eliminate that waste and focus your budget on households that actually hire contractors.

### Geographic Targeting

We define your campaign geography down to the zip code level. Your ads run only within your service area. If you cover a 30-mile radius, that is exactly where your impressions go. No wasted spend on markets you cannot serve.

### Demographic Targeting

We layer in demographic filters including age, household income, home value, and homeownership status. Your ads reach the people who own homes, have the financial capacity to hire a professional, and live in properties that need the services you provide.

### Behavioral and Interest Targeting

Spectrum Reach can target households based on viewing habits, purchase behavior, and lifestyle indicators. We can reach households that have recently searched for home improvement content, moved into a new home, or shown interest in categories related to your trade.

### Contextual Targeting

Your ads can be placed alongside relevant content categories. A [roofing](/industries/roofing/) commercial running during a home renovation show hits differently than the same commercial interrupting a sports broadcast. Contextual alignment increases engagement and recall.

Your campaign goes live across the selected streaming platforms and connected TV devices. From launch day forward, we monitor performance metrics and provide regular reporting.

**Metrics we track and report:**

- Total impressions delivered
- Unique household reach
- Average ad frequency per household
- Video completion rate
- Audience demographic breakdown

We do not launch a campaign and walk away. Active optimization means adjusting audience targeting, shifting budget between platforms, and modifying frequency caps based on real performance data. If one platform is delivering stronger completion rates than another, we reallocate budget accordingly.

Regular performance reviews keep you informed and ensure the campaign evolves as market conditions change.
