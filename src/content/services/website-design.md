## Two Ways to Build Your Online Presence

Every contractor has different needs. A one-truck [plumbing](/industries/plumbing/) operation does not need the same website infrastructure as a multi-location [HVAC](/industries/hvac/) company running seasonal promotions. That is why we offer two separate website design packages, each engineered for a specific set of business requirements.

### Custom Performance Sites

**Starting at $2,500 | Delivered in 3-4 Weeks**

Our Custom Performance Sites are built with Astro, a modern framework that ships zero JavaScript to the browser by default. The result is a website that loads in under 2 seconds on any device, scores near-perfect on Google's Core Web Vitals, and gives you a measurable ranking advantage over competitors still running bloated template sites.

This option is built for contractors who want a professional online presence without the overhead of managing a CMS. We handle all content updates for you. Your site stays lean, secure, and optimized at all times.

**What makes a Custom Performance Site different:**

- Zero client-side JavaScript shipped to the browser
- Sub-2-second load times on mobile and desktop
- Pre-rendered HTML pages that search engines can crawl instantly
- No database to hack, no plugins to update, no security vulnerabilities
- Hosting costs a fraction of what WordPress hosting requires

Speed matters more than most contractors realize. Google uses page load time as a direct ranking factor. Every additional second of load time increases your bounce rate by roughly 32%. When a homeowner searches "website design for plumbers" or "web design for contractors," the companies with fast, well-structured sites claim the top spots. The rest fight over scraps on page two.

Our Astro builds are especially effective for service businesses that need a clean, authoritative web presence without the complexity of regular content publishing. If you do not plan to blog weekly or update your site constantly, a Custom Performance Site delivers more value per dollar than any WordPress build on the market.

### WordPress Sites

**Starting at $4,000 | Delivered in 4-6 Weeks**

Our WordPress sites give you a full content management system with a custom theme designed specifically for your trade. You get complete editorial control over your pages, blog posts, service descriptions, and media galleries. Every WordPress build comes with hand-selected plugins, hardened security configuration, and a training session so you can manage your site with confidence.

This option is the right choice for contractors who want to publish content regularly, run seasonal landing pages, or manage multiple service locations through a single dashboard.

**What a WordPress build includes:**

WordPress powers over 40% of the internet for good reason. The ecosystem of plugins and integrations is unmatched. Need to connect your site to a CRM, add an appointment scheduling widget, or integrate with ServiceTitan? WordPress makes it possible without custom development work.

For HVAC companies running seasonal campaigns or plumbing outfits expanding into new service areas, a WordPress site provides the flexibility to spin up new pages, publish educational content, and adjust your messaging without waiting on a developer.

## Why Contractor Websites Fail (And How We Fix It)

Most contractor websites underperform for predictable reasons. Understanding those failure points is the first step toward building something that actually works.

### Problem: Template Sites That All Look the Same

Thousands of plumbing and HVAC websites use the same handful of themes. Visitors have seen these layouts before. They signal "generic" and "small-time" even when the contractor behind the site does excellent work.

**Our fix:** Every ServiceLine Pro website is designed from scratch for your specific trade and brand. No recycled templates. No shared design assets. Your site looks and feels like it belongs to a company that takes its reputation seriously.

### Problem: Slow Load Times That Kill Rankings

Page builders like Elementor and Divi add layers of bloated CSS and JavaScript that cripple page speed. A site that takes 5 or 6 seconds to load on mobile will hemorrhage visitors and rankings.

**Our fix:** Custom Performance Sites ship zero JavaScript. WordPress builds use a lightweight custom theme with clean code. Both options prioritize speed at every level of the stack.

### Problem: No Clear Path to Conversion

Many contractor sites bury their phone number in the footer, use generic "Contact Us" buttons, and include long forms that ask for too much information. Visitors get frustrated and bounce to a competitor.

**Our fix:** We engineer every page around a specific conversion goal. Phone numbers are prominent. Forms are short. CTAs are placed where eye-tracking data says visitors actually look.

### Problem: No SEO Foundation

A site without proper heading structure, meta tags, schema markup, and internal linking is invisible to search engines. It does not matter how good your site looks if nobody can find it.

**Our fix:** SEO is baked into the architecture from the first wireframe. We build sites that are ready to rank the moment they go live.

## The Web Design Process for Home Service Companies

We have refined our process across [hundreds of contractor website builds](/case-studies/). Every project follows the same four phases, keeping timelines tight and results predictable.

### Phase 1: Discovery and Strategy

Before we design a single element, we dig into your business. What services generate the most revenue? Which competitors are winning in your area? What does your ideal customer search for when they need help?

We audit your current online presence, analyze competitor websites, and define a site map that aligns with your business goals. This phase typically takes 3 to 5 business days and ends with a documented strategy you approve before we move forward.

### Phase 2: Design and Wireframing

Our designers create a homepage mockup and wireframes for your core interior pages. You see exactly what your site will look like on desktop and mobile before any development begins.

Revisions happen here, not after the site is built. This keeps the project on schedule and ensures the final product matches your vision. Most clients approve designs within one round of feedback.

### Phase 3: Development and Content

With approved designs in hand, our developers build your site with clean, semantic code. Simultaneously, our content team writes persuasive copy for every page, optimized for your target keywords and service areas.

We handle image sourcing, optimization, and placement. If you have photos from completed projects, we incorporate those alongside professional stock imagery that matches your brand.

### Phase 4: Launch and Optimization

Before going live, we run your site through a comprehensive checklist covering speed tests, mobile responsiveness, form functionality, analytics tracking, and SEO verification.

We handle DNS migration, SSL configuration, and hosting setup so the transition from your old site (or your first-ever launch) is seamless. Post-launch, we monitor performance for 30 days and make adjustments based on real user data.
